How it works
Up and running
in 3 steps

No bank visits, no spreadsheets, no trust issues. Just a link and a goal.

1
Create your group
Choose a group type, set your goal amount, billing cycle, and number of members. Takes 2 minutes.
2
Invite your people
Share your group link on WhatsApp. Members join, add their card once, and PayTribe handles every charge automatically.
3
Watch the money move
Auto-charges on due date. Failed payments retry automatically. When the goal is hit, funds go exactly where they should.
